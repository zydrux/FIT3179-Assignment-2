Vaccinations

These bubble charts show the percentage of vaccinations for specific diseases, against the total percentage that each country uses their money towards health. The size of the bubbles describes how many children under the age of 5 die per 1000 population. The deaths and vaccinations might not be related to the total expenditure, however it is somewhat related.
